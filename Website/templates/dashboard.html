Flipkart Grid 6.0

Flipkart Grid 6.0 is a national-level competition inviting undergraduate students to design a smart quality test system using camera vision technology. Participants will tackle real-world challenges in shipment quality and quantity assessment, with the chance to win cash prizes and internships. The competition offers networking opportunities and a platform to showcase technical skills to industry leaders. Key judging criteria include innovation, feasibility, and impact.
